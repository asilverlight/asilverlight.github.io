About

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China, fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhicheng Dou. Previously, I received B.Eng (2024) degrees in Artificial Intelligence from¬†Nankai University(NKU).

Currently, My research interests focus on

  • Agentic Reinforcement Learning
  • Deep Search Agents
  • Scientific Research Agent
  • Tool-Integrated Reasoning Agent

My long-term goal is to explore an automated, scalable, and safe way that fosters exceptional intelligence to achieve AGI.
